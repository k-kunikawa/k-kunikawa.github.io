Welcome to my homepage

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Mathematical Science, Faculty of Science and Technology at Tokushima University.

My research area is in Differential Geometry and Geometric Analysis. In particular, I am interested in Elliptic and Parabolic Partial Differential Equations of second order on Riemannian manifolds.
